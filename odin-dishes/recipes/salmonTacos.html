Salmon Tacos

Delicious salmon tacos made with corn tortillas, perfectly seasoned salmon, aioli sauce, cabbage, lime, and Tabasco!

I like to use a jumbo amount of salmon per mini t0aco. Make these guys extra thick!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon filets
  • 6-8 corn tortillas
  • 2 limes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • Toppings (optional, to taste): aioli sauce, cabbage, tobasco

Steps

  1. With a paper towel, pat the salmon dry.
  2. Pour 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil on salmon.
  3. Add the salmon to a mixing dish and combine the salt, black pepper, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder to the top of the filet.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the salmon skin side down and cook until the skin is crispy and browned, around 4-5 minutes.
  5. Flip the salmon and cook for another 3-4 minutes depending on thickness of filets.
  6. Remove salmon to a plate and let it rest and cool down for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Prep the tortillas by placing aioli sauce on each one.
  8. Cut the salmon and evenly distribute salmon amongst the 6-8 tortillas.
  9. Add cabbage to each taco.
  10. Add tobasco sauce to each taco.
  11. Lastly, squeeze lime on each taco and serve!
