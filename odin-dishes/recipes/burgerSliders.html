Burger Sliders

These mouthwatering tasty treats are a quick dinner night idea. Juicy bursting hamburgers on delicious buns!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 lb gound beef (80/20)
  • 1/8 lb cheese
  • 12 hawaiian sweet roles
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Toppings (optional, to taste): aioli sauce, lettuce

Steps

  1. Weigh out packed patties of 1.5 oz onto baking sheet.
  2. Season each pattie with the salt, black pepper, onion powder and garlic powder.
  3. Heat a large non-stick skillet to medium-high heat.
  4. Toast the 12 hawaiian roles until desired texture.
  5. Place patties on skillet and cook on each side for about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Flip patties over and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Lastly, place a piece of cheese over each pattie and cook until the cheese is melted on each pattie.
  8. Remove each pattie and place directly into each toasted hawaiian role bun.
  9. Add aioli sauce and lettuce on top of each pattie.
